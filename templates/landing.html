It Started With a Message

"URGENT: Don't open your windows tonight." A simple text, forwarded thousands of times in hours. By morning, a city was in panic. There was no emergency — just a ghost story designed to travel.

We watched how one lie, packaged correctly, could travel around the globe before the truth could even finish its first cup of coffee. That's why we created TruthGuard.

Step 1: The Fabrication

A sensationalist headline is generated by a bot or malicious actor, designed to trigger maximum emotional response.

Step 2: The Echo Chamber

Algorithms recognize the high engagement and push it to thousands of like-minded users who share it without verifying.

Step 3: The Impact

Real-world consequences occur — from store closures to medical misinformation — based on a document that never existed.
